### **Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) Setup in PGP**

-   **Important:** If your organization does not use Single Sign-On (SSO), you are **required** to set up Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) to access PGP. You will be automatically prompted to complete MFA setup during your first login, and you cannot proceed to the platform until MFA is configured.
-   If your organization uses SSO (Okta, Azure, PingID, Google, etc.), MFA is managed
