## Overview

Scan scheduling allows you to define specific time windows during which PGP will perform scanning activities. This feature helps you manage system resources effectively and ensures scans occur during optimal times for your organization.

## Setting Up a Scan Schedule

### Time Window Configuration

You can configure two key aspects of your scan schedule:

1.  **Days of the Week**: Select which days scanning should occur
2.  **Time Window**: Define the start and end times for scanning activities

### Time Zones

-   Scan schedules can be configured by time zone
-   Available options:
    -   UTC (Coordinated Universal Time)
    -   ET (Eastern Time)

## Schedule Management

### Creating a New Schedule

1.  Navigate to **Settings** in the main navigation
2.  Select the **Scan Settings** tab
3.  Locate the "Scan Schedule" section
4.  Configure your schedule:
    -   Select days using the radio buttons for each day of the week
    -   Choose start time from the dropdown menu (30-minute increments)
    -   Choose end time from the dropdown menu (30-minute increments)
    -   Select your timezone (UTC or ET) from the dropdown menu
5.  Click "Save" to apply the schedule

### Modifying an Existing Schedule

You can update your scan schedule at any time by:

1.  Adjusting the selected days
2.  Modifying the time window
3.  Saving your changes

### Deleting a Schedule

To remove a scan schedule:

1.  Navigate to the scan schedule settings
2.  Click the "Reset to Default" button

## Best Practices

### Optimal Scheduling

-   Schedule scans during off-peak hours to minimize impact on operations
-   Consider time zone differences if managing multiple locations
-   Ensure adequate time windows for comprehensive scanning

Setting up your scan schedule is an important step in optimizing your continuous threat exposure management with PGP. By configuring scans to run during appropriate time windows, you can ensure comprehensive coverage while minimizing impact on your operations.
